About Me

I am Andry Maulana Akbar, a student from Malikussaleh University. I took part in the 2nd Merdeka Student Exchange program in 2022. I was accepted at one of the leading universities in Indonesia, namely Gadjah Mada University. I am grateful to be able to join the Student Exchange Program and become one of the students

The Merdeka Campus Program provides has many benefits as well as various kinds of experiences in it, including making friends from all over Indonesia, being able to study directly on the Gadjah Mada University campus, and participating in the Nusantara Module activities by visiting historical places in Yogyakarta
